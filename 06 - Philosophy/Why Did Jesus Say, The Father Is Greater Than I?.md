## The Question

Muslims and skeptics quote John 14:28 constantly:
> *"The Father is greater than I."*

They claim this proves Jesus is not God — just a subordinate being.
This file destroys that reading using nothing but the grammar of the
sentence and the text of John itself. No external theology required.

---
